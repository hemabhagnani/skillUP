How To dig your way to success even in 2020

25 June 2020

About

In this time of crisis we came across many students who have doubts regarding what next? Amongst many internship opportunities being cancelled, no sign of job interviews, even examination being delayed, we all have found ourselves wondering how can we utilise this time?

A webinar on "How to dig your way to success even in 2020?" The main focus of this webinar will be essential software skills, how to develop them and job hunting. In this world of competition only working hard is not enough, one must work hard and smart in the right direction.

Our goal is to bring students together to give them an idea of how they can sharpen their tech-skills in this time of pandemic.

Our Speaker-Avikalp Kumar Gupta

Avikalp is a techie with a drive to solve problems. He currently works as a Full-Stack Engineer at GetMega, an up and coming gaming startup in Bangalore and is a mentor on Alokit Innovations. Before joining GetMega, Avikalp has worked with Microsoft Research as a Research Fellow and with Fresh Gravity as a Senior Consultant in Artificial Intelligence.

Avikalp completed his B.Tech. in Computer Science from IIT Kanpur. Some of his notable achievements include:

  • Selected for YSI Global program 2018 (24 selected out of 10,000+ applications worldwide) - and came out at 1st place among the 8 teams in the final pitch-day Earthpreneurs event.
  • 1st place in Industry category in Microsoft's annual global "//oneweek" hackathon 2016
  • KVPY Scholar (AIR 175, 2012)
  • AIR 99 in IIT-JEE 2012
